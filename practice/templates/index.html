Frequently Asked Questions

What is pothole detection and how does it work?

Pothole detection uses advanced algorithms and image processing to identify and analyze potholes on roads. Our system detects their location, size, and severity through uploaded images.

How accurate is the area calculation?

Our system leverages AI to ensure precise measurements, but accuracy can vary based on image quality and angle. For best results, follow our image upload guidelines.

How is the repair cost estimated?

We use industry-standard rates and materials data, along with the detected pothole area and depth, to calculate an estimated repair cost. This helps municipalities and contractors budget effectively.

What types of roads does the system support?

The system works on all types of paved roads, including asphalt and concrete surfaces. Ensure good lighting and clear visibility when capturing images.

Can I integrate this system with my city’s maintenance database?

Yes! Our platform offers API access for easy integration with existing infrastructure management systems.

Is my data secure when I upload images?

Absolutely. We employ advanced encryption methods to ensure that all uploaded images and user data remain secure and private.
